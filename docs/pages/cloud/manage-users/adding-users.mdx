# Adding Users

<Callout type="info">
    You must have the [role](user-roles) 'Owner' to invite others to your organisation.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Ensure the user you're inviting has an account.
To invite a user, they first must have successfully created an account.

### Open the [Organsiation Management](https://aerl.cloud/organization) page

![Manage Organisation](/manage-org-dropdown.png)

### Click **Invite** and enter their email

Once you have entered their email, click **Add**.

![Invitation Modal](/invite-modal.png)

### The user is now in the organisation
Now, if the user navigates to the [Organization Switcher](https://aerl.cloud/organization/switch), they should see the organization listed as an option.

</Steps>

After they have joined the organisation, you will be able to configure their [role](user-roles).
